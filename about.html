R&B Environmental Drilling, LLC

R&B Environmental Drilling, LLC is a woman-owned, family-run business that was founded in 2021 after seeing a need for a reliable, responsive, and reasonable environmental drilling service in the Minneapolis-Metro. With the custom direct push drill rigs purchased from Mobile and Environmental Sampling and Analysis, Inc (MESA), we use our 14+ years of combined consulting to mitigate common frustrations in working with sub-contractors and fulfill all your environmental drilling needs.
